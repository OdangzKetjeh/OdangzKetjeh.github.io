James Arthur

James Arthur is a singer and songwriter from England. He rose to fame after winning the ninth series of The X Factor in 2012. His debut single, a cover of Shontelle's "Impossible," was released by Syco Music after the final and debuted at number one on the UK Singles Chart. Since then, the single has sold over 2.5 million copies worldwide, making it the most successful winner's single in the show's history.

The Weeknd a.k.a Abel Tesfaye

The Weeknd lahir dengan nama Abel Tesfaye pada 16 Februari 1990, di Toronto, Kanada, the Weeknd telah menjadi salah satu penyanyi R&B alternatif terkemuka di industri musik. Dia adalah anak dari imigran Ethiopia tetapi memiliki sedikit kontak dengan ayahnya, yang meninggalkan keluarga ketika the Weeknd masih balita.
